About Us

Amongst The Top Best Eye Clinics That You Can Trust

Firmly established by a deeply rooted history and strong leadership

MANNA Eye Clinic was established in 2005 through a bequest of Dr Elizabeth Hertz to the Ministry of Health. From general eye care to comprehensive ophthalmic procedures, we are a non-profit specialty hospital consistently ranked at the top as one of the best eye hospitals in Cameroon.

Firmly established by a deeply rooted history and strong leadership, MANNA Eye Clinic is focused on providing world-class eye care to patients, driven by the need to protect sight and further our ever-growing efforts to advance eye research, this drive has let us to being ranked consistently amongst the best ophthalmology hospitals in Cameroon. Our team comprises some of the world's top eye doctors and has more top eye surgeons than any hospital in Cameroon.
